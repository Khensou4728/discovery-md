## 🛠 What this tool does

Product discovery often takes days of manual work. You must listen to audio, transcribe interviews, tag findings, and organize insights. discovery-md automates these tasks. It uses artificial intelligence to scan your documents. The tool maps your data to standard product management frameworks.

The software produces the following artifacts:

*   Structured interview guides based on your current project goals
*   Opportunity-solution trees to map potential features to user needs
*   Summaries that follow the Mom Test interview methodology
*   Categorized lists of pain points and user goals
*   Evidence-backed strategy documents

This approach keeps your team aligned on user needs. You spend less time moving sticky notes and more time talking to customers.

## 💡 Best practices for better results

The quality of your output depends on the quality of your input. Use these habits to get the most value from the tool:

*   Keep your interview transcripts clean. Remove filler words but keep the original user tone.
*   Clearly mark who is the interviewer and who is the research participant.
*   Group your files by specific interview batches.
*   Use the tool immediately after your interview sessions. This prevents memory bias from affecting your notes.
*   Update your opportunity-solution tree whenever you discover a new user problem.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems, check these items first:

*   The application does not open: Ensure you have the latest drivers for your graphics card. Try restarting your computer.
*   Process hangs: Verify your internet connection remains stable. If the screen goes blank, wait one minute for the process to recover.
*   File errors: Confirm your documents use a supported format like .txt, .md, or .csv.
*   Missing menu options: Reinstall the software using the original link to ensure all files are present.

You can close the application at any time by clicking the "X" in the top right corner. The software automatically saves your work before it closes, ensuring no data loss occurs between sessions.
